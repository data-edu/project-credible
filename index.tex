Welcome to the \textbf{Project CREDIBLE (Creatively Reimagining
Engagement With Data in Biology Learning Environments}) website.

Project CREDIBLE is an NSF-funded STEM education research project
associated with the \href{https://www.utk.edu/}{University of Tennessee,
Knoxville}, The \href{https://gsmit.org/}{Great Smoky Mountains
Institute at Tremont}, and
\href{https://dataclassroom.com/}{DataClassroom}.
